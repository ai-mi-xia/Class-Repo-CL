A tale untold...

“In eras of time long forgotten, a kingdom called Babylonia was ravaged by terrible and fearsome monsters called the Blight. It’s brave ruler and princess, Bel, was a priestess of the moon and a powerful sorceress, and she protected her people from the Blight with her Magic. But as time went on, she grew weary and fearsome, and the Blight grew stronger and stronger…”
